Extract data from paper forms with AI

FormExtractor is a service that transforms physical documents to digital structured data with AI technology.

Give it a go!

Pre-configured Extraction Models

Extraction models on general shopping receipts, identity cards and most of the official licenses are already available.

Multiple OCR Engines Supported

FormExtractor leverages the power of existing OCR services to deliver accurate results. Google Vision API, Azure Computer Vision OCR and Tesseract are currently supported.

Intuitive Web Portal

Users can submit forms, configure extraction parameters, label fields, add and manage existing models on our decorated web-based portal.

Mobile Camera Friendly

Uploaded photos are passed a set of optimazation processes including light and contrast adjustment, skew correction and edge detection to boost the extraction accuracy.
